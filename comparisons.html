Stress is not just a "feeling"—it is a physiological cascade managed by the HPA Axis (Hypothalamic-Pituitary-Adrenal). When the body remains in a state of chronic high cortisol, our systems of detoxification and nutrient absorption begin to fail.

To manage this, we look to the synergy between adaptogens and systemic support. Below is the clinical comparison of our library's key herbs and how they target different aspects of the stress response.
